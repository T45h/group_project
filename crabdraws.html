I Draw Too


As I an immensely caring, talented, (and modest) crab, I am most alturistic when it comes to taking the time to give back to my fellow critters, and thanks to my do-good-ing nature, I have pooled my creativity into a series of paintings which will be sold in aid of animals more endangered than myself.
(Although, that being said, a crab of my calibre is very rare to find, one might say I am the most endangered on the list...)
